# Tokenomics & Incentives

> The total supply of JMDT is hard capped at **1 Billion tokens**, ensuring a fixed and transparent monetary base for sustainable growth and decentralised governance.

The allocation is thoughtfully structured to incentivise participation, support long-term ecosystem development, and provide liquidity for market access.

---

## Token Overview

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| **Token Name** | JMDT (Jupiter Meta Data Token) |
| **Total Supply** | 1,000,000,000 (1 Billion) — hard cap |
| **Token Type** | Utility + Governance |
| **Primary Use** | DAG rollups, L2 commitments, node staking, DAO governance |

---

## Token Utility

JMDT powers every layer of the protocol:

- **DAG Rollups** — JMDT tokens are used to commit DAG state transitions to L2
- **L2 Commitments** — Fuel Sequencer-submitted zkRollup batches to Ethereum
- **Node Staking** — Validators and Seed Nodes stake JMDT to participate in AVC consensus and earn rewards
- **DAO Governance** — Token holders vote on protocol upgrades, parameter changes, and ecosystem grants
- **Enterprise Access** — Enterprises receive JMDT via private allocation and grants to operate L3 DAG nodes

## Token Allocation

### Distribution Breakdown

| Pool | Allocation | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **L3 Developments, Enterprises & Early Adopters** | 55% | Incentivise L3 applications, enterprise integrations, DAG-based data contributors, testnet validators, and ZK-proof participants. Anchors network utility and network effects. |
| **Founders and Promoters** | 25% | Designated for the original visionaries and promoters behind the JMDT protocol. Locked and vested to reflect long-term alignment with the ecosystem's mission. |
| **Founding Team and Employees** | 10% | Allocated to the core team responsible for engineering, cryptography, business, and operational execution. Subject to long-term vesting schedules. |
| **R&D and Foundation Initiatives** | 10% | Reserved for protocol-level research, zero-knowledge innovation, cryptographic upgrades, and grants to academic or research institutions building on JupiterMeta. |

---

## Vesting & Unlock Strategy

To protect long-term network integrity and reduce sell pressure:

- All team, promoter, and investor tokens are subject to **cliff-based linear vesting**
- Early adopter tokens are released in **phased batches** over a 6–12 month horizon
- The Foundation's deployment of ecosystem grants and treasury tokens is governed by internal frameworks

---

## Incentive Design

This allocation model ensures the **JMDT token remains utility-focused**, incentivises genuine contribution, and unlocks sustainable value for all stakeholders:

- **Developers** — grants and ecosystem rewards for building on JMDT
- **Data contributors** — rewards for participating in the ZK-verified data economy via SuperJ and enterprise DAGs
- **Validators** — staking rewards for honest AVC consensus participation
- **Enterprise partners** — private allocations for deploying L3 DAG infrastructure

The largest pool (55%) directly supports **real-world adoption** — the greatest signal of long-term protocol health.
