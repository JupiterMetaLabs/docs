Tokenomics & Incentives

The total supply of JMDT is hard capped at 1 Billion tokens, ensuring a fixed and transparent monetary base for sustainable long-term growth.

The allocation is thoughtfully structured to incentivise participation, support long-term ecosystem development, and provide liquidity for market access.

Token Overview​

PropertyValue
Token NameJMDT (Jupiter Meta Data Token)
Total Supply1,000,000,000 (1 Billion) — hard cap
Token TypeUtility
Primary UseDAG rollups, L2 commitments, enterprise access

Token Utility​

JMDT powers every layer of the protocol:

  • DAG Rollups — JMDT tokens are used to commit DAG state transitions to L2
  • L2 Commitments — Fuel Sequencer-submitted zkRollup batches to Ethereum
  • Enterprise Access — Enterprises receive JMDT via private allocation and grants to operate L3 DAG nodes

Token Allocation​

Distribution Breakdown​

PoolAllocationPurpose
L3 Developments, Enterprises & Early Adopters55%Incentivise L3 applications, enterprise integrations, DAG-based data contributors, testnet validators, and ZK-proof participants. Anchors network utility and network effects.
Founders and Promoters25%Designated for the original visionaries and promoters behind the JMDT protocol. Locked and vested to reflect long-term alignment with the ecosystem's mission.
Founding Team and Employees10%Allocated to the core team responsible for engineering, cryptography, business, and operational execution. Subject to long-term vesting schedules.
R&D and Foundation Initiatives10%Reserved for protocol-level research, zero-knowledge innovation, cryptographic upgrades, and grants to academic or research institutions building on JupiterMeta.

Vesting & Unlock Strategy​

To protect long-term network integrity and reduce sell pressure:

  • All team, promoter, and investor tokens are subject to cliff-based linear vesting
  • Early adopter tokens are released in phased batches over a 6–12 month horizon
  • The Foundation's deployment of ecosystem grants and treasury tokens is governed by internal frameworks

Incentive Design​

This allocation model ensures the JMDT token remains utility-focused, incentivises genuine contribution, and unlocks sustainable value for all stakeholders:

  • Developers — grants and ecosystem rewards for building on JMDT
  • Data contributors — rewards for participating in the ZK-verified data economy via SuperJ and enterprise DAGs
  • Enterprise partners — private allocations for deploying L3 DAG infrastructure

The largest pool (55%) directly supports real-world adoption — the greatest signal of long-term protocol health.
